# The Small Business AI Revolution Series - Digital Catalog

> **Practical AI implementation guides for small business owners who want to leverage artificial intelligence without needing a PhD in computer science or a Silicon Valley budget.**

---

## 📚 About This Series

The Small Business AI Revolution Series is a comprehensive collection of books designed to help small business owners understand, implement, and benefit from artificial intelligence. Written by **Adaryus Gillum**, this series demystifies AI and provides practical, actionable guidance for businesses of all sizes.

**The AI revolution isn't coming—it's here.** This series empowers small business owners to join this revolution with confidence, transforming their operations, customer service, marketing, and financial management through intelligent automation.

---

## 🎯 Who This Series Is For

- Small business owners curious about AI but overwhelmed by technical jargon
- Entrepreneurs looking for practical AI solutions on a budget
- Business leaders who want to stay competitive in the intelligence age
- Service providers, retailers, and professionals seeking operational efficiency
- Anyone who wants to separate AI reality from marketing hype

### Foundation (Books 1-3)
