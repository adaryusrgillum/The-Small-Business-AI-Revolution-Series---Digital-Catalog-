## Notes

### About Version Numbering
This project uses [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/):
- **MAJOR version** (1.x.x): Significant content additions or major restructuring
- **MINOR version** (x.1.x): New books, chapters, or features
- **PATCH version** (x.x.1): Bug fixes, typos, minor improvements

### About Book Numbering
The series uses non-sequential book numbering:
- Books 1-11: Core foundational and operational content
- Books 12-15: Marketing & Sales (planned for v1.1.0)
- Books 16-19: Customer Experience and Finance
- Books 20+: Industry-specific and advanced topics (planned)

This numbering allows for thematic grouping while leaving room for future expansion.

### Future Directions

The series will continue to grow with:
- **v1.1.0**: Marketing & Sales books (12-15)
- **v1.2.0**: Additional Finance and Data books (20-21)
- **v1.3.0**: Security and Ethics books (22-27)
- **v1.4.0**: Industry-specific implementations (28-33)
- **v1.5.0**: Advanced topics (34-37)
- **v2.0.0**: Complete series with interactive features

### Contributing

We welcome contributions! Future changelog entries should follow this format:
